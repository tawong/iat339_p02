Oatmeal

About this dough

Hearty and Healthy! Who can forget nostalgic oatmeal cookies paired with delicious rasins?

Quick Facts

Oatmeal is heart healthy. More than 37 scientific studies show that eating oatmeal daily may help reduce the risk of heart disease

  • Seventy-five percent of U.S. households have oatmeal in their cupboard.
  • Oatmeal cookies are the #1 non-cereal usage for oatmeal, followed by meatloaf and fruit crisp.
  • Oats were one of the earliest cereals cultivated by man.
