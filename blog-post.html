Frost It

Event: June 2017

Icing cookies doesn't have to be hard! We invited Icing Specialist, Lyndsay Sherman, to show us the basics.

Icing Made Simple

Skill Level:

Beginner / Intermediate

Class Size:

20 max.

What you'll learn:

  • Make dough and bake cookies
  • Decorate cookies with royal icing basics, like outlining and flooding, and create unique marbled designs
  • Decorate cookies for year round holidays like Christmas cookies, Valentine's Day cookies, Easter Cookies and Halloween Cookies

Class Description

Make classic sugar cookies from scratch with the Cookie Craft method. Not only will Lyndsay show you how to make the dough and cut out shapes, but she also shares expert tips for decorating the cookies with royal icing.

This class is perfect for anyone who wants to up their holiday baking skills and majorly impress their friends at the next cookie exchange.
