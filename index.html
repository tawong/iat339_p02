Core Brand Concepts

About Cookie Craft

Cookie Craft is a small-batch Canadian artisan cookie company that specializes in making fresh customized cookie dough that is all-natural, organic, corn syrup-free, with nut-free and gluten-free options. We opened our very first brick-and-mortar shop in 2005, and have been handcrafting cookie confections made from premium ingredients from local farms across British Columbia ever since.

Who We Are

Cookie Craft is fun, quirky, and adventurous! We’ll never say no to a flavour combination, so you can be as creative as you want with what goes into your cookie dough.

Why We're Different

Save time and hassle with ready-made cookie dough, which you can customize to your hearts delight! We offer unique ingredients that we’ve worked extra hard to source just for you (vary by season).

The process is simple: drop by the shop, pick your ingredients, and we’ll whip up as much cookie dough as you need. Then, drop by our toppings bar to sprinkle on your cookies, and sit tight as we package your dough. All the instructions are found on the package, and we’ll even throw in cookie craft idea to accompany your unique recipe.
