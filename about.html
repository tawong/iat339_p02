Our Story

Cookie Craft is a small-batch Canadian artisan cookie company that specializes in making fresh customized cookie dough.

How We Started

We opened our very first brick-and-mortar shop in 2005, and have been handcrafting cookie confections made from premium ingredients sourced from local farms across British Columbia ever since.

Who We Are

Cookie Craft is fun, quirky, and adventurous! We’ll never say no to a flavour combination, so you can be as creative as you want with what goes into your cookie dough.

What Makes Us Different

Save time and hassle with ready-made cookie dough, which you can customize to your hearts delight! We offer unique ingredients that we’ve worked extra hard to source just for you (vary by season).
